Loop & Downtown Core

Tourism has rebounded and weekday foot traffic is rising. Hotel renovations and riverfront upgrades continue to open opportunities for vendors and services.

  • Office occupancy: ~75–80%
  • Riverwalk visits trending up
  • Hospitality reinvestment is steady

South & Lakefront

Corridors around Hyde Park and South Shore are seeing reinvestment. Transit and cultural anchors power new storefront openings.

  • Building permits signaling rehab cycles
  • Strong park and lakefront draws
  • Opportunities in neighborhood services

West Side Logistics

Industrial corridors and freight access keep logistics healthy. City contracts support streets and sanitation operations.

  • Jobs in fleet & facilities grow with demand
  • Warehouse retrofits and permits active
  • Service leads around arterial streets

North Side Innovation

From River North to Ravenswood, tech and creative firms lift demand for professional services and commercial buildouts.

  • Strong pipeline of small permits
  • Growing need for specialty trades
  • Restaurant refresh cycles continue
